About StashBuddy

Welcome to StashBuddy, your comprehensive solution for managing student life during semester breaks. We understand the challenges of moving belongings and finding convenient services, and we're here to make things easier with three key features:


1. Thrift Store: Discover a marketplace where students can buy and sell essential items like buckets, blankets, and more. Whether you are looking to find a bargain or declutter, our thrift store offers a convenient platform to exchange goods within the campus community.


2. Storage Spaces: Need a safe place to keep your belongings during semester breaks? Our storage space directory connects you with local residents who offer storage solutions. Find and book secure storage options conveniently close to Thapar University, ensuring your items are safe and accessible when you return.


3. E-Rickshaw Directory: Navigate campus and nearby areas with ease using our e-rickshaw directory. Access a list of e-rickshaw services, complete with contact numbers, to make your commute smooth and hassle-free.


At StashBuddy, we aim to simplify your student life by providing practical solutions in one place. Explore our features and enjoy a stress-free break!
